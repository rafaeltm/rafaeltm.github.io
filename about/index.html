Who am I?

I hold a degree and a master’s degree in Computer Science. I felt interest and eagerness for technological issues from a very young age, and my passion for computers became my ambition. Ever since, I have had a clear passion for security and user privacy , and this devotion is the main reason that made me enroll in a PhD program in these areas of expertise.

My capabilities are dynamic, as I have a high adaption capacity to work in different environments whilst performing at a high productivity rate. Moreover, I believe I am more than capable to take decisions without hesitation to bring projects to a successful conclusion.

Finally, I have experience both doing teamwork and working independently. I always give the best of myself in what I do.
